About The Founder

Mr. Kibe is an ambitious, industrious, and focused young man. A problem-solver who has comeout strongly to address the unemployment challenges facing the youth and underprivileged segments of women groups. The Affluent Farmer's founder believes that the only thing mainly standing between the less privileged groups and their better living standards is information gaps,specifically on agriculture. This view is the sole purpose that pushed him to start Affluent Farmers- to light a candle forthose is the dark through sharing of limited, but invaluable agribusiness information to the lessprivileged groups in our society.
